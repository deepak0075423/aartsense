Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start on my project?

For most projects, we can begin within 1‚Äì2 weeks of agreeing scope and receiving a deposit. Rush projects can sometimes be accommodated ‚Äî please enquire and we'll do our best to fit your timeline.

Do you work with clients remotely / outside the UK?

Absolutely. A significant portion of our work is done remotely with clients in India, USA, UAE and beyond. We use video calls for creative discussions and deliver files digitally. For studio bookings, clients travel to our England facility.

Who owns the music once it's created?

It depends on the arrangement. For bespoke commissioned work, the client typically receives full copyright upon final payment. For collaborative projects, co-publishing arrangements can be discussed. We'll be transparent about rights from the outset.

How many revision rounds do I get?

This varies by package ‚Äî our Essential package includes 2 revision rounds, Professional includes unlimited revisions within scope. We find most projects are finalised within 2‚Äì3 rounds regardless, as we front-load the brief and creative process.

Can I bring my own musicians to the studio?

Yes, absolutely. Our studio welcomes external musicians. We can also arrange session musicians for you if needed ‚Äî we have a trusted network of professional instrumentalists across multiple genres.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes ‚Äî we offer a free 30-minute consultation call for new clients. This gives us a chance to understand your project and advise on the best approach before you commit to anything.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in whatever formats your project requires ‚Äî WAV (24-bit/48kHz or 24-bit/96kHz), MP3, AIFF, stems, MIDI, OMF/AAF for picture lock. Broadcast and streaming specifications are matched precisely.
