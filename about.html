Meet Arjuna

Arjuna is an award-winning music composer of Indian origin, now based in England, United Kingdom. With a portfolio exceeding 500 projects, he has established himself as one of the most versatile and sought-after composers working across film, television, and advertising today.

His musical journey spans continents ‚Äî from the rhythmic heartbeat of Indian classical music to the sweeping orchestral traditions of Western cinema. This unique cross-cultural fluency gives AartSense its signature sound: music that feels both universal and deeply personal.

Arjuna's compositions have graced blockbuster advertising campaigns, independent short films, documentary features, and wedding anthems remembered for a lifetime. Every note is crafted with intention, every arrangement tells a story.
